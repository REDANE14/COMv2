1.3 UTP Cable

Instruction

Tips: When making a CAT6 cable, use high-quality cable and the right tools, such as a cable cutter/stripper, crimping tool, and RJ45 connectors, to ensure proper termination and optimal performance. Be sure to follow TIA/EIA 568B.2 standards for the wiring configuration.

  1. First prepare your UTP Cable and Rj45
  2. Strip off the outer jacket of UTP Cable
  3. Separate your wire pairs
  4. Arrange wires according to white/orange, solid orange, white/green, solid blue, white/blue, solid green, white/brown, solid brown.
  5. Make sure that it will fit in to the connectors.
  6. Insert wires into connector
  7. Crimp the connector
  8. Test your cable
