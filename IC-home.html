About

In this course, we will explore the fundamentals of computing, from the basic principles of computer science to the latest technologies that are transforming our world. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced user, this course will provide you with a solid foundation in computing that will prepare you for further study or for using computers in your everyday life..

Throughout the course, we will cover a wide range of topics, including computer hardware, software, operating systems, computer networks, and programming. We will also discuss the social and ethical issues surrounding computing, including privacy, security, and the impact of technology on society.
